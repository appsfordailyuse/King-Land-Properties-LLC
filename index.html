Buyer's Guide

UAE Real Estate Legal Framework

Understanding the UAE legal system builds confidence. King Land guides you through every step — RERA registration to DLD transfer.

🏛️
RERA
Real Estate Regulatory Agency — governs all real estate in Dubai. All our developers are RERA registered and certified.
📋
DLD
Dubai Land Department — registers all property transactions. Buyers pay a 4% DLD fee upon transfer of ownership.
🔒
Escrow Protection
Off-plan developer funds are held in RERA-approved escrow accounts. Funds release only as construction milestones are met.
📝
SPA (Sales & Purchase Agreement)
The legal contract between buyer and developer. King Land assists with all SPA review and documentation throughout the process.
🔑
Oqood Registration
Dubai's off-plan property registration system. Mandatory at time of booking — protects buyers legally from day one.
🌍
Freehold for All Nationalities
All nationalities can own freehold property in UAE designated zones. No restrictions on foreign ownership of our featured projects.
UAE Golden Visa
Invest in Property · Get UAE Residency
Property investment in UAE can qualify you for long-term UAE residency through the Golden Visa program. Multiple projects in our portfolio meet the minimum investment threshold.
