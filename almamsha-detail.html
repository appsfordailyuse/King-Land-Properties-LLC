About Al Mamsha

The First Fully
Walkable Community

Al Mamsha is Sharjah's new destination and the first fully-walkable community in Sharjah that integrates modern living, retail and leisure into a vibrant urban and tech-savvy environment.

The community is divided into 3 zones: Al Mamsha Souks (Zone 1), Al Mamsha Seerah (Zone 2), and Al Mamsha Raseel (Zone 3) — each offering a unique living experience within a connected, walkable master-plan.
