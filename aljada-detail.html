About Aljada

The Downtown
of New Sharjah

Aljada is a landmark development that redefines Sharjah living. It is a destination where everything is at your fingertips — living, working, entertainment, recreation, shopping and culture all come together in a vibrant, unique urban environment spanning 24 million square feet.

Designed to house 75,000 residents upon completion, Aljada features the world-famous Madar entertainment hub designed by Zaha Hadid Architects, welcoming over 120,000 visitors a month. Smart homes, green parks, schools, hospitals, retail — all in one place.
